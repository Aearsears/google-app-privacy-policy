# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 11, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Aearsears Creator Tools application ("Application", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Application.

The Application is a personal tool designed to automate the publishing of YouTube Shorts to your YouTube channel. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand what information we collect and how we use it.

## 2. Data Collection

We collect only the minimum amount of data necessary to operate the Application and fulfill its intended purpose. Specifically, we collect:

### 2.1 Google Account Information

- Your Google account identifier used for OAuth authentication
- Email address associated with your Google account

### 2.2 YouTube Channel Information

- YouTube channel ID and channel name
- Channel metadata necessary to identify and access your channel

### 2.3 Video Metadata

- Video titles, descriptions, and tags
- Video content information (thumbnail data, video format details)
- Publishing timestamps and scheduling information
- Video visibility settings (public, unlisted, private)

### 2.4 OAuth Tokens and Credentials

- OAuth access tokens used to authenticate API requests
- OAuth refresh tokens to maintain authorized sessions
- Token scope information necessary for API operations

### 2.5 Application Usage Data

- Logs of publishing actions performed through the Application
- Error logs and diagnostic information
- Timestamps of API calls and Application interactions

## 3. Google API Services Usage

The Application uses the following Google APIs to function:

- **YouTube Data API**: To access, manage, and publish videos to your YouTube channel
- **Google OAuth 2.0**: To securely authenticate your Google account and obtain necessary permissions

We access only the YouTube API scopes necessary to publish Shorts to your channel. We do not request or access any additional user data beyond what is required for this specific function.

## 4. How We Use Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

- **Authenticating your identity**: OAuth credentials are used to verify that you are authorized to access the Application
- **Publishing content**: Video metadata and YouTube channel information are used to publish Shorts to your YouTube channel as directed
- **Maintaining application functionality**: We use publishing logs and diagnostic data to ensure the Application operates correctly
- **Troubleshooting and debugging**: Error logs help us identify and fix technical issues
- **Your explicit requests only**: We perform actions on your YouTube channel only when you explicitly request them through the Application

We do **not** use your data for:

- Marketing or advertising purposes
- Profiling or analytics beyond what is necessary for operation
- Selling, trading, or renting your data to third parties
- Any purpose not explicitly stated in this policy

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Where Data is Stored

- OAuth tokens and credentials are stored securely on your local machine or authorized devices only
- Application logs are stored locally with your consent
- No data is transmitted to external servers beyond what is required for Google API operations

### 5.2 Security Measures

- OAuth tokens are stored using secure storage mechanisms provided by your operating system or application framework
- Tokens are never logged in plain text
- All communication with Google APIs uses encrypted HTTPS connections
- Access to stored credentials is restricted to the Application only

### 5.3 Data Retention

- OAuth tokens are retained only as long as necessary to maintain the Application's functionality
- You may revoke Application access at any time through your Google Account settings, which will invalidate all stored tokens
- Application logs are retained locally and can be deleted by you at any time
- No backup copies of credentials are maintained beyond what Google's systems require

## 6. Data Sharing

**Your data is not shared with third parties.**

The only data transfers that occur are:

- **To Google**: OAuth tokens and API requests are sent to Google's servers to authenticate your identity and perform API operations on your YouTube channel
- These transfers are necessary for the Application to function and comply with Google's Terms of Service

We do not:

- Sell or trade your data
- Share your data with analytics companies or advertisers
- Transfer your data to other third-party services
- Use your data for any purpose beyond operating the Application

## 7. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates with the following third-party service:

### Google Services

- **Google OAuth 2.0**: Used for authentication
- **YouTube Data API**: Used to publish content to your YouTube channel

These services are subject to Google's own Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy), and we recommend reviewing it to understand how Google handles your data.

No other third-party services have access to your data.

## 8. User Rights and Data Deletion

### 8.1 Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Know what data we collect about you
- Request deletion of your data from the Application
- Revoke the Application's access to your Google account at any time
- Understand how your data is being used

### 8.2 Revoking Access

To revoke the Application's access to your Google account:

1. Go to https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
2. Find the Aearsears Creator Tools Application
3. Click the Application and select "Remove access"

This action will immediately invalidate all OAuth tokens and prevent the Application from accessing your Google account or YouTube channel.

### 8.3 Deleting Local Data

To delete all Application data stored on your device:

- Delete all Application configuration files from your local machine
- Delete any stored OAuth tokens by revoking access through your Google Account settings
- Remove any application logs or cache files

## 9. Google API Services User Data Policy

Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.

### 9.1 Limited Use Commitment

The Application will:

- **Only use data** for the explicit purposes described in this policy (publishing Shorts to your YouTube channel)
- **Not use data** to improve, develop, or market other services or products
- **Not transfer data** to third parties for any purpose other than operating the Application as directed by you
- **Not combine data** from Google APIs with data from other sources for purposes other than providing the Application's core functionality

This commitment ensures full compliance with Google's Limited Use requirements and protects your privacy in accordance with Google's policies.
